Volleyball

Volleyball is the game I enjoyed playing the most in my entire childhood

I have participated at inter school tournaments, and other small tournaments too

The game consists of a few techniques: Passing,Serving,Setting,Blocking and Digging

To become a pro one should master these techniques

Drills:

Wall drills: Bump, set, and dig against a wall to improve hand positioning and control

Pepper: A back-and-forth warm-up involving bump, set, and spike to build coordination

Target serving: Aim at marked spots on the court to build accuracy

Reactive drills: Partner tosses or cones for moving to hit low balls (angle digs)
